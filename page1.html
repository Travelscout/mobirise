Xenon 4 Sport

    XENON 4 SPORT is a great introduction to the world of aviation. This professional machine meets the German standards set by DAEC Civil Aviation Authority allowing the Xenon to pertain to the 560 kg category MTOW DAEC Kennblatt No. 601208.1. When designing the Sport model, we tried to keep the best combination - simplicity of workmanship, quality and reliability of used materials. The Xenon 4 Sport with its affordable price and low cost of ownership is ideal for both flying schools and individual clients. Thanks to its lightweight, proven and reliable ROTAX ULS (100 hp) engine it showcases excellent volatility and low fuel consumption 98/98 oct. For demanding customers we also offer the Xenon 4 Sport with engine CA ULST 135 PS. The cabin, like the whole XENON family, is made of carbon built in one monolithic piece like what you find in Formula One cars. This design offers excellent protection for the user as it acts as a robust safety cage. The newly designed instrument panel is equipped with professional analogue instruments with the ability to connect the GPS system.The new cabin offers more space, comfort and a perfect working environment for the pilot and a great resting place for the passenger. Combined with high performance and improved aerodynamics, the XENON 4 Sport can be successfully used for long distance travel, where it also helps with tailored luggage space and tailor-made bags adorned with the Xenon logo. Comfortable, ergonomic and heated seats made from top-grade materials combined with a great cabin finish ensure the pleasure of traveling at any time of the flight. All Xenons are manufactured in an industrial plant meeting strict ISO9001 standards
